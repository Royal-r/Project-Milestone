About Me

My Background

Hi there, I'm Royal, and I'm delighted you stopped by my website. I'm a Kennesaw State University senior working on an information technology degree. In my academic career, I have grown to have a strong enthusiasm for the dynamic and always-changing technology field. The potential for creativity and its boundless possibilities never cease to amaze me. I have a solid background in IT, and I can't wait to use what I've learned to build valuable solutions.

My Experiences and Skills

With a diverse skill set gained from various roles such as team member, customer service representative, porter, and front desk representative, I have developed customer service, adaptability, multitasking, teamwork, problem-solving, time management, communication, and writing skills. With these skills, I am ready to make a meaningful impact in the IT industry.

The chance to safeguard and preserve sensitive data in the digital era inspires me. My long-term goal is to be a highly trained cybersecurity specialist in a demanding and exciting workplace. My short-term goals include getting the necessary credentials and gaining real-world experience through internships or entry-level jobs. I'm dedicated to learning emerging technology and security procedures. As my career develops, I hope to assume leadership positions, create blogs to help other upcoming cybersecurity analysts, help create cutting-edge security solutions, and have a beneficial influence on protecting systems and data from cyberattacks.
